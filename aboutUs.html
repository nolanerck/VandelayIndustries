ABOUT US


Cast Images is a full-service licensed talent agency representing models, actors and kids. Established in Northern California in 1991, Cast Images aggressively pursues career growth for our talent and excellent service for our clients.

WHAT WE DO


Cast Images develops and books talent for television, print, voice over, film and runway. We also work diligently as mother agency for our fashion models, guiding and developing their national and international careers while always keeping our focus firmly on what is best for each individual personally and professionally.

We are always actively searching for new faces as well as experienced talent to join our agency. We strive to fill the needs of our local, national and international clients with amazing talent, attention to detail, and with the utmost of integrity.

OUR COMMUNITY


Cast Images has deep roots in Sacramento. For over twenty years we've been committed to supporting our local community and believe strongly in all the good things life in Sacramento has to offer. While we work intently on national and international growth, and especially on serving our clients in the San Francisco Bay area, our hearts are in midtown Sacramento. One of our greatest joys is bringing new clients to this wonderful city.

By contributing 5% of Cast Images' profits every year since we opened in 1991, we are proud to share the rewards of our business with charities in our community.

OUR MISSION


Good work with good people.
